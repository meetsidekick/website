Why You'll Love Sidekick

A Companion with Character

Watch your Sidekick react to motion and touch, displaying its "mood" on a vibrant OLED screen. Every interaction is unique, bringing life to your workspace.

Infinitely Customizable

Dive into its open-source hardware and software. Install custom firmware, add new features, and program unique behaviors. Your Sidekick, your rules.

Connected & Smart

Built with an ESP32, Sidekick boasts Wi-Fi and Bluetooth, opening up a world of possibilities for smart integrations and creative projects.

Portable Fun

Designed to be carried with you, Sidekick is ready to bring a smile wherever you go. Compact, durable, and always entertaining.

For The Nerds

(We love you). Here's what powers the SIDEKICK.

Processor

ESP32 dual-core with Wi-Fi and Bluetooth

Display

0.96" OLED high-contrast screen

Sensors

ADXL345 3-axis accelerometer

Audio

Integrated buzzer for feedback

Connectivity

Wi-Fi 802.11 b/g/n + Bluetooth 4.2

Power

USB-C charging with battery management
